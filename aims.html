Our Aims

The face value of FindFix UK

Why We Do What We Do

In identifying leaks quickly and accurately we hope to minimise any access required to reveal the leak. In acting promptly to repair or stop the leak we hope to minimise the risk of long term water damage and satisfy yourself that there are no further leaks within your property. Where other types of escape of water or moisture ingress are identified (other than a leaking pipe) we aim provide guidance and recommendations so that subsequent remedial works will be the most cost effective solution; we believe that for-armed (with knowledge) is for-warned (financially!).

A Plumber? A Surveyor? A Builder?...

Who to choose when trying to resolve damp or water ingress affecting your property, the cause of which is not clear? We exist to provide an alternative to you seeking various tradespersons, at various costs, in the hope that the right person may help…Our technicians are grounded and trained in all three backgrounds, all linked by one factor; damage to properties by water or moisture. We have over ten years’ experience in both locating and repairing leaks as well as advising insurance companies on the sources of water or moisture damage for insurance validation purposes. We always provide a fully documented report outlining our findings, the extent of the damage and our recommendations, suitable for presentation to your insurer.
